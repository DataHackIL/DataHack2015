The Jobs Page!


We get a lot of questions from friends and participants about job hunting in the data science field. While we have no intention of becoming an HR firm, we thought a single page with a simple table would be a nice service for the community.

Want to post a position to the page? Mail the company name (no anonymous posts) - which might incorporate a link the a jobs page, mail-to link for applications or just the company's website - the name of the position ("Data Scientist", "Machine Learning Research Expert", etc.), and a short description of the position (up to 350 characters; we inforce that) to jobspage@datahack-il.com.

Note that we will only post positions that are related to one (or more) of the following fields: data science, machine learning and statistics.

About

DataHack

DataHack is a community for data-enthusiastic programmers, designers and researchers in Israel. We aim to bridge academia and industry and help share precious knowledge and experience gained from work in machine learning, statistics and data science generally in joint events and online communication forums, and to bring together the Israeli data community.

Events & Community

Besides hosting a yearly hackathon at the Hansen House, Jerusalem, we also organize the DataTalks meetup series; read all about these efforts below. You can also find us on twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn, and even sign-up to our monthly-ish newsletter.

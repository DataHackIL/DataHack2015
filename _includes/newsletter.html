The DataHack newsletter is an easy way to get a curated list of data science and machine learning related news and events, including other events by DataHack, like the DataTalks meetup series, straight to your mail box. We will send out an issue about once a month.

Signing Up

It's easy! Just fill out this short form, and you'll get our next issue. Past issues can be read here. Also, if you've got an interesting and data-science-related article, paper, event or piece of news, mail us at newsletter@datahack-il.com, and we'll try and include it in our next issue.
